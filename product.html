All Purpose Fertilizer

Benefits Details Application

This all-purpose fertilizer is formulated to support healthy plant growth for flowers, vegetables, trees, and shrubs. Rich in essential nutrients, it helps boost foliage, strengthen roots, and promote vibrant blooms.

Our fertilizer is a balanced mix of nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium (N-P-K 10-10-10) designed for optimal plant nourishment. It is made from high-quality, eco-friendly ingredients, ensuring safe application for both home gardens and larger agricultural projects.

For best results, apply the fertilizer evenly to soil around plants and water thoroughly. Use 1-2 cups per plant or 1kg per 10 square meters of garden space. Reapply every 4-6 weeks for sustained growth and health.
