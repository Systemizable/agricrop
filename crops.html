Welcome to our seasonal journey in farming and gardening!

Explore the unique challenges and joys each season brings as we cultivate our land and nurture our plants. Hover over each square to learn more about the distinct characteristics of winter, spring, summer, and fall. Click on any square to dive into its timeline and discover how we embrace the rhythm of nature throughout the year.

Winter Season

Winter is a time of resilience and quiet growth. While many gardens rest, we cultivate hardy crops like winter greens and root vegetables. This season allows us to focus on both harvesting and planning for the upcoming year. Discover how we make the most of winter’s unique offerings and prepare for a vibrant spring.

Click on the square labeled Winter to see the winter planting timeline and learn more about what you can grow during the colder months.

Spring Season

Spring is a time of renewal and growth. As the soil warms and days lengthen, we prepare for planting, nurturing seeds that will blossom into a vibrant garden. Discover how we embrace this season’s energy and start our journey.

Click on the square labeled Spring to see the spring planting timeline and learn more about what you can grow during the warmer months.

Summer Season

Summer brings warmth and abundance. Our gardens flourish under the sun, and it’s a busy time of harvesting and maintaining. Learn about our summer practices and how we care for our crops during this peak growing season.

Click on the square labeled Summer to see the spring planting timeline and learn more about what you can grow during the hotter months.

Fall Season

Fall is a season of transition and harvest. As we gather the fruits of our labor, we also prepare the land for winter. Explore our fall activities, from collecting produce to planting cover crops that enrich the soil.

Click on the square labeled Fall to see the spring planting timeline and learn more about what you can grow during the cooler months.
